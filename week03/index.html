The School of Engineering and Technology offers two study abroad opportunities in its Global IT Program.

Global IT Management: India

The Global IT Management course teaches students how to think and behave as global managers of IT-related projects, including working with geographically diverse teams, cultural issues in the workplace, and how to incorporate a global perspective into IT projects that will reach an international audience.

The Global IT Management study abroad includes:

  • Collaboration with Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology in Pune to allow students to engage with their Indian peers and produce a viable IT project
  • Interviews with leaders of international corporations with operations in India to learn about what it's like to work with the US from an Indian perspective
  • Tourism to vital historic sites that introduce students to the thousands of years of Indian cultural heritage

Global IT Citizenship: China

This program will provide students with a wide scope of Global IT Citizenship, exploring issues related to multicultural integration within global companies and concerns about citizens working and studying abroad. It includes an in-depth cultural exchange where students will collaborate with students from China throughout the course.

Our study abroad program will consist of the following key elements:

  • Visits to multinational and Chinese companies to learn about employment opportunities for foreign citizens
  • Collaboration with Shenzhen University, where students will be able to interact with Chinese students and collaborate on an IT project across the globe
  • Visits to a variety of cultural and historical sites to help the students understand the history and culture of China, both past and present
